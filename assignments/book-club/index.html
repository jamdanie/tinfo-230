Welcome!

Welcome to Code & Prose—our book club for people who love the craft of building things and the stories behind them. We meet to discuss books that explore technology, design, creativity, and the human side of code. Whether you’re brand-new to web design or you’ve been building for years, you’re welcome here.

What We're Reading

Title: The Web Designer: A Story of HTML, CSS, and the Big City
Author: Paul McFedries
Pages: 232
Summary: A beginner-friendly, story-driven introduction to the building blocks of the web—HTML for structure and CSS for style—framed through a fun narrative that makes learning feel less like a lecture and more like an adventure.
